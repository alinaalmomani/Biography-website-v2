• Promoting digital skills among the youth of Jordan, Orange Jordan launched the Orange Community Digital Centers umbrella covering the Kingdom • Reaching the top, Orange Jordan ranked first in Jordan and sixth regionally in providing digital services through mobile phones. • BIG by Orange summit which brought together 200 entrepreneurs from Jordan, Orange FAB from countries around the world, and investors from various sectors. • Orange Data Center became the first data center in Jordan to be awarded the prominent «Tier III» certificate. • Digital transformation of operations such as Orange Jordan to be first operator in Jordan to use AI in Chatbot, provide an e-SIM service, and a wide range of services dedicated to businesses (B2B). • Increasing showrooms to 63 across the Kingdom, 13 of which are smart shops. • Enabling easier services and inquiries and saving time through the fully updated “My Orange” App, used by more than 300,000 customers. • Inaugurating the Orange Coding Academy, the first of its kind in the Middle East. • Embracing “Training for Employment” across Orange digital centers in 14 locations and entrepreneurial programs that have helped 37 startups since its launch in 2015. • The official inauguration of the mega 3 Orange Solar Farms with a total capacity of 37 MW. • Ensuring inclusion of persons with disabilities by introducing sign language at Orange shops and launching the “Differently Abled, Definitely Enabled” umbrella. • Raising awareness of the responsible use of technology in Jordan by launching “The Gift” campaign. • Aiming to increase the company’s efficiency and enhance customers’ relations, the company inaugurated new customer service call centerin abdaly and karak. • As a responsible digital leader, we supported Jordan during the Covid pandemic by donating JOD 1.26 million and offered free browsing of the official school education platform “Darsak”, and free data bundles for all government universities’ students. • Seamless transition to working from home during lockdown, while ensuring business continuity, and service delivery. • To expand our digital programs by implementing the Innovation Space project, we received a grant from the European Union, and launched Orange Digital Center (ODC) under the partnership between Orange Foundation Jordan and GIZ. • Towards enhancing and expanding our coverage, we introduced the latest technology and biggest 27000 KM fiber optic network in the Kingdom. • Launching Orange Money e-wallet. • My Orange users reached more than 660,000 users. • In line with our environmental responsibility, Orange Jordan’s Solar Farm project covered more than 60% of the company’s energy needs. • Launching the environmental campaign, “Committed to Preserving Our World”. • Launching the first Mobile FabLab in Jordan with Shamal Start to provide youth in governorates with digital fabrications skills. Recognized as one of the as one of the first companies to apply international certificate standards in the kingdom, by obtaining the Customer Operations Performance Centre’s certificate (COPC).
